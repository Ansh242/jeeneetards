About Us

Jeeneetards is not your typical coaching cult. We are the outliers, the misunderstood, the ones who got tired of hearing “Just do NCERT and pray.” Born from the chaos of JEE and NEET prep, we are battle-scarred droppers, repeaters, and rebels who’ve faced pressure, isolation, and the crushing weight of expectations and still chose to fight.

We don’t flex ranks, we expose lies. We believe in strategy over syllabus, mindset over marks, and raw truth over polished garbage. This space is for those who are done being lost in the noise and ready to move with clarity.

If you’re looking for hope, this isn’t it. If you’re looking for fire, welcome home.
